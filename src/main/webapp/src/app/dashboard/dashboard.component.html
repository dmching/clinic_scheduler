Athletic Training Clinic Description

Injuries are unfortunately a part of participation in intercollegiate athletics. Texas Lutheran University recognizes that fact and in order to assist the student-athlete in continued participation or safe return to his or her chosen sport has in place a team of healthcare providers ready to administer care when necessary. The primary goal of the athletic training staff is to provide fast and comprehensive patient-centered medical care.

Student-athletes may seek care for injury or illness through multiple venues at TLU. The athletic training clinic located in Rinn Fieldhouse is available for care on a daily basis and is provided by athletic trainers. Most athletic practices, depending on injury risk, have an athletic trainer on site or can reach an athletic trainer in the clinic if required. If a student-athlete needs to see an orthopedic surgeon or general practitioner, appointments can be made around class schedules with physicians at their respective offices. Also available to the student-athlete is the student health center which houses an onsite registered nurse and either a nurse practitioner or physician two times a week.
